R Alan is an American artist living in Rochester Hills, MI. His early life began in Chicago, and he went on to study at Michigan State University getting his Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering.

He has most of his work showcased at a private location in Southeast Michigan. He plans to one day put his work in the Chicago Museum of Contemporary Art.

Apart from adding to his renowned art collection, he enjoys researching biology, playing the guitar, and packing his wife lunches.
